music:
- always loop
- one at a time
- can be cancelled by consequence music command
voice:
- can be cancelled by consequence voice command
- optionally can be cancelled by next textbox command, or next 2nd text command
sound:
- loop sound can be cancelled by next loop sound
- normal sound can be cancelled by next normal sound
- normal sound optionally can be eventually cancelled by consequence command (maybe 2nd text command)
